# Product Mission

## Problem

Centro Escolar CEICAVS tracks attendance, blog communications, and administrative tasks through manual and paper-based processes. This creates inefficiency, data loss risk, and no centralized visibility for staff or students.

## Target Users

A single school — Centro Escolar CEICAVS — with three roles:

- **Admin:** Full platform control (users, groups, attendance, blog, tools, transcription)
- **Teacher/Docente:** Classroom-focused (own groups, mark attendance, blog posts, teaching tools, AI transcription)
- **Student/Estudiante:** Consumer-level (view attendance, read blog, access tools)

Not designed for multi-tenant or multi-school use.

## Solution

A custom-built school management platform tailored to CEICAVS's specific workflows, with:

- **Spanish-first UI** designed for non-technical staff
- **AI-powered audio transcription** for class recordings — a capability off-the-shelf tools don't offer
- **Simple, focused interface** — no bloat from generic school ERP features
- **Role-based experience** — each role sees only what they need
